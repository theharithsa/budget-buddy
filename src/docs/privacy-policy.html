1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Google Account Data: Email address, name, and profile picture (via Google OAuth)
  • User Preferences: Theme settings, view modes, and app configurations
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for PWA functionality

Financial Data

  • Expense Records: Amounts, categories, descriptions, dates, and optional receipt images
  • Budget Information: Budget limits, categories, and time periods
  • Analytics Data: Spending patterns and trends derived from your input
  • Achievement Progress: Gamification scores and milestone tracking

Usage Analytics

  • App Interactions: Feature usage, page views, and user flows
  • Performance Data: Load times, error rates, and technical metrics
  • Session Information: Login frequency and session duration

Important: We only collect data that is necessary to provide and improve FinBuddy's services. All financial data remains under your complete control.

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Service Provision

  • Storing and organizing your financial data securely
  • Generating personalized insights and analytics
  • Providing AI-powered spending analysis and recommendations
  • Synchronizing data across your devices
  • Enabling achievement tracking and gamification features

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve features
  • Monitoring system performance and reliability
  • Debugging technical issues and optimizing user experience
  • Developing new features based on user needs

Security and Compliance

  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity or security threats
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations
  • Maintaining audit trails for security purposes

What We DON'T Do: We never use your personal financial data to train AI models, create user profiles for advertising, or sell insights to third parties.

4. Data Security and Protection

Encryption and Storage

  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • At-Rest Encryption: Firebase uses AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Secure Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 with industry standards
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with principle of least privilege

Security Monitoring

  • 24/7 automated threat detection and response
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Real-time monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Incident response procedures with notification protocols

Data Backup and Recovery

  • Automated daily backups with geographic distribution
  • Point-in-time recovery capabilities
  • Disaster recovery procedures tested regularly
  • Data integrity checks and validation

Security First: FinBuddy meets or exceeds industry standards for financial data protection, including SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 compliance through our Firebase infrastructure.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Essential Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential app functionality:

  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you signed in securely
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your theme, view mode, and settings
  • Security Cookies: Prevent fraud and protect against attacks
  • PWA Storage: Enable offline functionality and data caching

Analytics and Performance

We use monitoring tools to improve service quality:

  • Dynatrace Monitoring: Performance metrics, error tracking, and user experience data
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymized usage patterns and app performance
  • Session Recordings: Anonymized interaction patterns to improve UX

Your Cookie Choices

  • You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences
  • Disabling essential cookies may limit app functionality
  • Analytics cookies can be disabled without affecting core features
  • Clear cache and cookies option available in user profile menu

Cookie Consent: By using FinBuddy, you consent to our use of cookies for essential functionality and performance monitoring. No personal financial data is included in analytics tracking.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Data Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access all your information through the app interface
  • Export Data: Download your expenses, budgets, and analytics
  • Edit Information: Modify or correct any data at any time
  • Delete Data: Remove specific expenses, categories, or entire account

GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Restrict: Limit how we process your data

CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to Know: Information about data collection and use
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

Easy Data Management: Most privacy rights can be exercised directly through your FinBuddy account settings. For additional requests, contact us at privacy@inspirilabs.com.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Active Account Data

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Data is kept to provide continuous service and maintain your financial history
  • You can delete specific data items at any time
  • Exported data allows you to maintain personal backups

Account Deletion

  • Immediate Deletion: User-accessible data is removed immediately
  • Backup Purge: Data is removed from all backups within 90 days
  • Analytics Anonymization: Aggregated analytics are anonymized
  • Legal Retention: Some records may be kept for legal compliance (anonymized)

Inactive Accounts

  • Accounts inactive for 3 years receive deletion warnings
  • Final deletion occurs after 3 years and 6 months of inactivity
  • Multiple notification attempts via email before deletion
  • Option to export data before account removal

8. International Data Transfers

FinBuddy uses Firebase (Google Cloud) infrastructure, which may store and process data in various locations worldwide. All international transfers are protected by:

  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate data protection
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved contracts for data protection
  • Certification Programs: SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other security certifications
  • Google's Global Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade security regardless of location

Regional Preferences: While we cannot guarantee data locality, Firebase automatically optimizes for performance and compliance within your region.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

FinBuddy is not designed for or directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If We Discover Child Data

  • We will immediately delete any information from children under 13
  • We will terminate any accounts created by children under 13
  • We will notify parents/guardians if we become aware of unauthorized use
  • We will review our procedures to prevent future occurrences

Teen Users (13-17)

  • Teens may use FinBuddy with parental supervision
  • We recommend parental involvement in financial education
  • Parents can request access to or deletion of teen's data
  • Enhanced privacy protections apply to users under 18
