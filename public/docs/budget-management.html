Budget Styles Explained

Conservative

Higher essential spending, lower discretionary spending. Focus on savings and security.

Balanced

Even distribution between essentials and lifestyle. Good for most people.

Aggressive

Lower essential spending, higher lifestyle spending. More flexible approach.
