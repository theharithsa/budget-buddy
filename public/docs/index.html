Frequently Asked Questions

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! FinBuddy uses enterprise-grade Firebase security with end-to-end encryption. Your data is isolated per user and we cannot access your personal financial information.

Can I use FinBuddy offline?

Yes! FinBuddy is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works offline. You can track expenses without internet and sync when connected.

How does the AI analysis work?

Our AI uses GPT-4 to analyze your spending patterns and provide personalized recommendations. It has fallback modes for reliable analysis even without internet.

Do budgets reset every month?

Yes! Budget limits remain the same each month, but spending calculations automatically reset. You can view historical performance using the month selector.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export charts and reports from the analytics dashboard. Full data export features are planned for future releases.

Is FinBuddy free to use?

Yes! FinBuddy is completely free and open-source. All features including AI insights are available without cost.
